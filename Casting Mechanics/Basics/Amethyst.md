To begin hex casting, you must first find an amethyst geode. These are abundant in the world, and Hex Casting increases the frequency of them. Once you have found one, mine some clusters and hope to the gacha gods that you get some [Charged Amethyst](https://hexcasting.hexxy.media/v/0.11.3/1.0/en_us#items/amethyst@charged). Once you have some, create a staff and progress to [[Drawing]].
